NHS daily bed occupancy, general and acute, England, 2017-present

Since March NHS England has published daily updates on overnight hospital beds occupied by Covid and non-Covid patients. The former is updated daily; the latter monthly. In previous years only a quarterly average for bed availability and occupancy was published: we obtained the 2017-19 daily data through a Freedom of Information request to NHS England. There is a small break in the data coverage in 2020 between November 12th and November 17th.

You can see a regional breakdown of this data here

Source: NHS England Overnight Beds Open and Occupied (Excel download) and NHS England Covid-19 Hospital Activity.

NHS daily beds open, England, 2017-present

Since March NHS England has published daily updates on overnight hospital beds open. This number includes occupied and unoccupied beds. In previous years only a quarterly average for bed availability was published: we obtained the 2017-19 daily data through a Freedom of Information request to NHS England.

Source: NHS England Overnight Beds Open and Occupied (Excel download) and NHS England Covid-19 Hospital Activity.

Independent sector daily bed occupancy, England, 2020-present

Since April NHS England has published daily updates on overnight hospital beds occupied by Covid and non-Covid patients in the independent/non-NHS sector. This is the first time the NHS has collected non-NHS occupancy data, so data from previous years is not available.

Source: NHS England Covid-19 Hospital Activity.

Daily potential Covid-19 telephone triages and completed online assessments NHS pathways, England

This dataset shows the total number of NHS Pathways triages through 111 and 999, and the total number of online assessments via NHS 111 online, where either has received a potential COVID final disposition since 18 March 2020.

Source: Potential Coronavirus (COVID-19) symptoms reported through NHS Pathways and 111 online, NHS Digital.
